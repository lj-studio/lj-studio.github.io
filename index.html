About Us


Established on 2012 by Lisi and Janne, L & J Studio & Gallery is a creative space in Second Life, a vast 3D-generated virtual world and platform filled with user-generated content where people can interact with each other in real-time.

In addition to create 3D avatars and 3D buildings, L & J also hosts regular art exhibitions and events, showcasing the work of local and regional artists. The gallery features a diverse range of art styles and mediums, including painting, drawing, sculpture, and mixed media.

Whether you are looking to develop your artistic skills, meet other artists, or simply appreciate the beauty of art, L & J Gallery is a great place to visit.
